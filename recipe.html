Tomato Bocconcini

Tomato bocconcini will keep you warm in the cooler months, and it's ready in a flash.

RATE THIS RECIPE

Preparation:   10 min     Cooking:   10 min     Serves:   4  

Ingredients
Instructions

Ingredients

  • 375g dried penne pasta
  • 400g tomato medly mix
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, crushed
  • 1 1/2 tabespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon caster sugar
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • 180g tub bocconcini cheese, drained, torn

Instructions

Step 1

Cook pasta in a large saucepan of boiling salted water, following packet directions until tender. Drain.

Step 2

Meanwhile, cut half the tomatoes in half. Heat 2 teaspoons of oil in a large, deep frying pan over a medium-low heat. Add garlic. Cook for 1 minute or until fragrant. Add tomato. Cook fr 4 - 5 minutes or until just starting to collapse. Add vinegar, sugar and remaining oil. Toss to combine.

Step 3

Add basil, cheese and pasta to tomato mixture. Season with salt and pepper. Toss to combine. Serve.
